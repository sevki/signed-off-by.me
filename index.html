Signed-off-by?me

tl;dr get me to fix

Welcome to signed-off-by.me, you are probably here because you forgot to sign-off atleast one commit.

What does that mean?

By sigining-off your commits you make the following declaration.
Simply put, by signing off your commits you are telling the maintainers that to the best of your knowledge the patch you, or a third party, has created is able to be distributed under a compatible open-source license amd it does not infringe on copyrighted material.

Fix

If it's your last one, you are in luck, just do 
git commit --amend -s --no-edit
and do a force push. If you have more then one commit thats not signed-off
git filter-branch --msg-filter "cat - && echo && echo 'Signed-off-by: Your Name '" HEAD
and do a force push.
